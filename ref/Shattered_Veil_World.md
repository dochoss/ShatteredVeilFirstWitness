The Shattered Veil is a world defined by a cosmic struggle between preservation and entropy. On one side is Scaelithar, the Radiant Dragon God of Twilight, who represents memory, transition, and light. On the other is Vorthuun, the Abyssal Maw, an entity of cosmic unraveling that hungers for silence and the erasure of identity. The metaphysical foundation of memory in this world comes from the Eidolarch, an impossibly ancient, slumbering proto-deity buried beneath the industrial forge-city of Cindralis. The world's magic is heavily tied to Memorystone, a crystalline substance formed from the echoes of forgotten gods that can store thoughts and divine resonance.

Here is a summary of the important events your party—Tsuuky, Pants, Yakhov, Tanis, and Jasper Moonfallen—has witnessed so far:

*   **The Mystery in Dragonrest:** The adventure began at the Rusty Lantern Tavern, where the party met the proprietor Garrick Stonebrew and the barmaid Elara Swiftfoot. Directed to the House of the Silent Flame, the group met Father Annon and investigated a glowing memorystone tablet that had been mysteriously marked with a rune of the Maw. During this investigation, they discovered a strange piece of cloth with a slight evil presence that inexplicably changed color from red to blue. 
*   **The Trail into Whispergrove:** After a young girl named Ryn witnessed a villager walking blankly into the forest trailing green light, the party followed the clues into the mist-laced Whispergrove. At a crossroads, they discovered a pack of kobolds using a corrupted amulet to strip mind-wiped thralls of their belongings and march them deeper into the woods. 
*   **The Hollow Verge and Elthara Vey:** The trail led to the Hollow Verge, a corrupted ritual site where Veythra Marr—an emissary of the Maw—performs the Unmaking Ritual to strip victims of their memories and turn them into faceless "Nullborn". Here, the party met Scholar Elthara Vey, who explained that corrupted memorystone is being used like a blade to sever people from their own identities.
*   **The Maw's Luck:** While crossing the water to the Crystal Caverns, the party was intercepted by a ship called *The Maw's Luck*. They battled Captain Ryllan Vaerris, a man corrupted by Veythra Marr (whom he called the Crimson Woman), who used an abyssal amulet to project a terrifying mindflayer illusion over himself.
*   **The Singing Vault:** Upon reaching the Crystal Caverns, the group escorted Elthara Vey into a highly unstable chamber of humming memorystone. As the resonance of the room threatened to tear reality apart, the party fought off echo-drunk scavengers to protect Elthara while she successfully extracted a Pure Memorystone Fragment.
